FOREST MOUSE: FUN 3D ADVENTURE GAME FOR ANIMAL SIM FANS

Welcome to Forest Mouse, a delightful 3D adventure game. This game invites you to step into the tiny paws of a gray mouse living in a magical forest and cozy cottage. This enchanting animal simulation game challenges you to survive, gather resources, craft a snug burrow, and build a thriving mouse family. Ready to scamper through a world of wonder? Let’s explore what makes this game a must-play adventure game!

DIVE INTO A FULL ANIMAL SIM ADVENTURE

Your mission is to collect food like nuts, berries, and cheese; gather materials like hay and twigs; and craft a cozy burrow to call home. As you progress, you’ll face challenges like dodging predators, managing hunger, and completing quests to level up. With 3D visuals and relaxing gameplay, Forest Mouse is perfect for fans of animal simulator games and anyone seeking a cozy adventure game experience.

How to Play

  • Move: Arrow Keys / WASD
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Eat: E
  • Pick up/Drop: F
  • Attack: Q
  • Click "Quest Help" anytime you need a hint on what to do next!

Pro Tips for Thriving in this Game

  • Focus on Food Early: Stockpile nuts and berries to keep your energy high for long exploration sessions.
  • Prioritize Quests: Completing quests earns experience points and unlocks rare resources.

Customize Your Mouse with Unique Skins

  • Visit the main menu to access a variety of unlockable skins, each with special traits.
  • Choose Home Mouse, a pet version that even gets along with cats!
